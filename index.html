Snowboarding Electrified.

We developed an electrically-powered snowboard.



Boredom to Idea

For adventure seekers and winter sports enthusiast who are looking for adrenaline rush without the high cost of ski hill lift tickets, the eBoard is an electric self-powered snowboard that enables a person to ride on flat, downhill and even uphill terrain without breaking a sweat. Unlike other snowboards, our product is motor-driven and doesnâ€™t rely on gravity to propel the person forward. The high torque density electrical drive systems makes the eBoard significantly lighter and more quiet compared to other gas powered snowboards, while the eBoard is powerful enough to achieve speeds upwards of 25 mph on flat terrain. The eBoard uses a six point track suspension allowing the track to maintain better grip as the rider uses toe and heel edging to carve through a variety of snow conditions.

Specifications

What we know based on current testing:

  • Maximum speed: 40 mph unloaded (64.37 kph)
  • Forward/reverse full speed operation
  • Motor: brushed DC electric, providing 4.5 Hp (3.36 kW)
  • Battery: Lithium cell, providing 364 W*hr
  • Runtime: 30-45 minutes
  • Throttle hand control
  • Interfaces with standard ski/snowboard boots
  • Weight: 29 lbs (13.15 kg)



Product Features



Track suspension: The eSnowBoard incorporates a front, central, and rear suspension providing superior grip in a variety of different snow conditions. The suspension is also capable of pitching up to 30 degrees to allowing the track to maintain contact with the snow as the rider uses heel and toe edging.



Track Tension: A central track tensioner keep a constant tension on the top and bottom sections of the track.


LCD screen: An on-board color LCD screen provides a readout of power consumption, track speed, throttle percentage, and battery voltage.


Regenerative braking: During braking the electrical motor can be used as a generator to charge the battery. This feature is particularly useful for braking on larger declines as the energy generated is significantly more compared to decceleration on flat ground.


Safety

  • SafeStart: The eSnowBoard has a safe start feature requiring the throttle to be zeroed before the motor will be engaged.
  • Controller disconnect: If the controller is disconnected or the control signal is lost, the power to the motor is shut off.
  • Ramped acceleration: The throttle is ramped to ensure safe acceleration of the rider.
